### Key Takeaways

* Always begin with enumeration before exploitation.
* Verify the VPN interface before launching attacks.
* Captured authentication hashes can often be cracked offline.
* Strong passwords and protocols such as NTLM require careful handling during assessments.
* WinRM provides remote administration capabilities and is a common post-exploitation access method in Windows environments.
